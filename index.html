A lil about me ...

I'm a total goof-ball who loves life and is so excited about continuing to learn to code. I completed three amazing months at General Assembly's Web Development Immersive Program, where I completed full stack projects and broke code constantly. From there, I decided I wanted to focus more on Front-End Development so I took some additional classes this summer. I loved it so much that I'm currently an Expert In Residence at General Assembly for their evening Front End Development classes, where I assist students with their projects and homework. When I'm not doing that, I love to watch documentaries, make jewelry, travel, paint, do yoga and my all-time favorite activity is cake decorating.

This is a picture of me playing outdoor skeeball at one of the opening parties of the World Domination Summit, an incredible conference in Portland, OR that I've gone to for the past three years. It's the most inspiring weekend of the year and is a constant reminder for me to follow my dreams and passions.
